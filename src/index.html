seconds

Action Count

  • Your Tamagotchi has 5 stats, each with a max value of 30 that decreases by 1 per second.
  • Take care of your Tamagotchi by clicking the various action buttons. Each click will add 5 points.
  • If your Tamagotchi falls below 100 total stat points, it will become sick, doubling the rate of point decrease.
  • If the food level drops to 0, your Tamagotchi will die, and if the love level drops to zero, it will run away.
  • The objective is to keep your Tamagotchi alive and affectionate for you for as long as possible, good luck!
